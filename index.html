About

I was born an American Citizen in Corona, California, living with a father, a mother, and two sisters. Foothill Elementary was the school I started in. When I came to elemantary, found myself having trouble with reading comprehension and some math. In the end, I manage to resolve those issues when I completed 6th Grade.

Citrus Hills was the middle school where my philosophy, mentality, and thinking changed drastically due to the environment I was being educated in. Despite bullying, gossip and social issue being some of the negative factors on my education, I manage to improve my studies during my time in middle school. Math and Language Arts (Literature and Writing) were the most important to use students. At this time, I took interest in story writing and later game development. However, coding wasn't introduced to me until High School.

High school has kind of changed me, seeing that story writing and art are just my hobbies. Animation and Game Development were now the focus of my career. My social skills were still trying to improve, but instead of bullying and gossip, I now had to deal with family tensions, financal management, driving education, and college preparations. And all this was overwhelming to me, and today I still feel that sense of anxiety when dealing with college.

After graduating, I left So far, the University of Corilleras just reminded me of High School with a few exceptions. My education here, however, is more challenging. My social skills were kind of sent back to square-one since I had difficulties speaking Tagalog (and it still regret not getting to learn the language). Other than social issues, family issues and financial management have not left me. The ethics here left me with mixed responses; some teacher were fair, some were unfair, and some were something inbetween.
